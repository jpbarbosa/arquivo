ESTABLISHMENT

Professor MANOEL DE OLIVEIRA FRANCO SOBRINHO, with practicing advocacy since 1936, after holding a number of nationally relevant public offices, founded in 06/17/83 the firm OLIVEIRA FRANCO ADVOCACIA S/C, which later became OLIVEIRA FRANCO, RIBEIRO, KÜSTER, FERRAZ & ADVOGADOS ASSOCIADOS, with constitutive act filed in Ordem dos Advogados do Brasil - Seção do Paraná, under number 15 of Book "A" and pages 100 and 102 of Book "B", on August 17, 1983.
