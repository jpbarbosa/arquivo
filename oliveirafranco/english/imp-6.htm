ESTABLISHMENT

Professor MANOEL DE OLIVEIRA FRANCO SOBRINHO, with practicing advocacy since 1936, after holding a number of nationally relevant public offices, founded in 06/17/83 the firm OLIVEIRA FRANCO ADVOCACIA S/C, which later became OLIVEIRA FRANCO, RIBEIRO, KÜSTER, FERRAZ & ADVOGADOS ASSOCIADOS, with constitutive act filed in Ordem dos Advogados do Brasil - Seção do Paraná (Brazilian Bar Association - PR Section), under number 15 of Book "A" and pages 100 and 102 of Book "B", on August 17, 1983.

OPERATIONAL AREAS

Divided in three basic areas, the firm works with specific services. In the pre-litigious service, elaborates legal opinions and prepares consultations, is prepared to orientate in negotiations, elaborate contracts, prevent litigation and insure the execution of obligations. In litigation services, works in all stages of a law suit. Keeps associations with Brazilian and foreign law firms.

Business Area


Constitutional and Administrative Law: political and citizenship rights, public works, licitation, administrative contracts. Commercial and Societary Law: opening of companies, split-up, merger, meetings, shareholder's agreement, companies registration, franchising. Tax Law: taxes, tributes, contributions. Industrial Law: authorship, marks and patents. Environmental Law: prevention and recovery of rights.
