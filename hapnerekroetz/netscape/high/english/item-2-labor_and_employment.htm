LABOR AND EMPLOYMENT

The lawyers in the labor and employment law group have a broad range of experience in exclusive representation of management for all aspects of labor and employment law matters.

The group serves our clients in many different ways and represents a national basis of clientele. Primary goals are to minimize the risks of litigation, represent clients in administrative agency matters concerning various employment-related issues and to maintain non-union status.

The lawyers conduct training sessions for supervisors of client companies in the areas of employment discrimination laws, maintaining non-union status, conducting lawful and proper interviews and performance evaluations and other employment-law issues. Additionally, members of the group represent clients in negotiation, arbitration and proceedings before state administrative agencies.

Also, clients are represented in federal court litigation under various discrimination laws, employee benefit laws, wage and hour laws, safety and health laws and other employment related matters.

The litigation group represents clients at both trial and appellate levels in state and federal court throughout the south of Brazil. These include virtually every type of general business litigation, as well as complex commercial litigation. The section also handles arbitration and mediation proceedings.

The section represents our clients in litigation involving mergers and acquisitions, contracts, securities, finance, civil and criminal antitrust violations, specialized criminal defense, dissolution of businesses. The group also represents clients in matters concerning professional and product liability, including construction litigation and architect's and engineer's liability, libel and slander, civil rights, real estate, health care, environmental regulations, zoning, ad valorem taxation and eminent domain.

Working with other sections of the firm, the lawyers represent clients in patent, trademark, copyright and other intellectual property litigation, bankruptcy and insolvency matters and international business litigation.
